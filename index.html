Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

CameraPOS collects the following information to provide our point-of-sale service:

  • Account data: Username, email address, and hashed password
  • Business data: Business name, product inventory, and sales records
  • Device data: Device type and operating system for app compatibility
  • Payment data: M-Pesa transaction references (we do not store payment credentials)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the CameraPOS service
  • To sync your inventory and sales data across devices
  • To process M-Pesa STK push payment requests
  • To send password reset emails when requested
  • To manage your subscription via RevenueCat

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Railway cloud servers hosted in the EU. We use industry-standard encryption (bcrypt for passwords, HTTPS for all data in transit). Locally on your device, data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database.

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except as described in this policy.

4. Third-Party Services

CameraPOS uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — subscription management. See RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Safaricom M-Pesa — payment processing. See M-Pesa Privacy Statement
  • Resend — transactional email delivery for password resets
  • Expo / EAS — app distribution and updates

5. Camera Access

CameraPOS requests access to your device camera solely for scanning and identifying products in your inventory. Camera images are processed locally or sent to our secure vision API for product recognition. We do not store or share camera images.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and business data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us. Sales records may be retained for up to 7 years for accounting compliance purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your business data

8. Children's Privacy

CameraPOS is designed for business use and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
