Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

CameraPOS is a camera-based point-of-sale application designed specifically for Kenyan businesses. We help business owners and their teams manage inventory, process sales, handle M-Pesa payments, and comply with Kenya Revenue Authority eTIMS requirements — all from a single app.

This policy explains what data we collect when you use CameraPOS and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

Account information: When you create a CameraPOS account, we collect your business name, username, and email address. Your password is stored as a one-way hash and can never be read by us.

Business data: Product names, prices, stock levels, categories, and product images you add to your inventory. Sales records including items sold, totals, payment methods, and the cashier who processed each sale.

Team accounts: Usernames and roles (admin or cashier) of team members you add to your business account.

M-Pesa data: Your M-Pesa business shortcode and transaction references for STK push payments. We do not store customer phone numbers beyond the immediate payment request.

eTIMS data: Your KRA PIN and business details required for eTIMS compliance registration.

Camera usage: When you scan products using the camera, images are sent to our secure vision API for product identification. These images are processed and immediately discarded — we do not store or share them.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide your business with a fully functional point-of-sale system
  • To sync your inventory and sales data securely across devices and team members
  • To process M-Pesa STK push payment requests on your behalf
  • To identify products via camera scan using AI vision
  • To support eTIMS compliance with Kenya Revenue Authority
  • To send password reset codes to your registered email address when you request them
  • To manage your CameraPOS subscription

4. Data Storage & Security

All CameraPOS business data is stored on secure cloud servers. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text. All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

On your device, inventory and sales data is stored locally in an encrypted SQLite database so the app works offline. This data is synced to the cloud automatically when you have an internet connection.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or business data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

CameraPOS integrates with the following services to deliver core functionality:

  • Safaricom M-Pesa — processes STK push payment requests. Customer payment data is handled directly by Safaricom. See the M-Pesa Privacy Statement.
  • Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) eTIMS — receives business registration data for tax compliance as required by Kenyan law.
  • RevenueCat — manages in-app subscriptions on iOS and Android. See the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.
  • Resend — delivers transactional emails such as password reset codes. Only your email address and the reset code are shared.

6. Offline Mode

CameraPOS is designed to work without an internet connection. When offline, all sales and inventory changes are saved locally on your device. When your connection is restored, data is automatically synced to the cloud. No data is lost during offline periods.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account and business data for as long as your account remains active. Sales records are retained for a minimum of 7 years in compliance with Kenyan tax and accounting regulations.

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below. Note that sales records required for legal compliance may be retained even after account deletion.

8. Your Rights

As a CameraPOS user you have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal and business data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information on your account
  • Request deletion of your account and non-compliance data
  • Export your sales and inventory data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of CameraPOS after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
